Device Advice

Always read the instructions and take time to read the terms and conditions before accepting them.

During set up, change default passwords and create a strong and memorable password.

Disable voice purchasing, this will prevent hackers from purchasing items from your account.

If possible, set up a Two Factor Authentication as an additional layer of protection.

Check for software and app updates. If there is an option for automatic updates enable them, this saves you the worry!

Visit the manufacturer's website to check for any updates or guidance.

Be sure to check on privacy and government websites offering guidance for consumers.

Speakers will listen to their 'wake word', and doorbells watch you. If you want complete privacy or are worried about a vulnerability, simply turn it off!
